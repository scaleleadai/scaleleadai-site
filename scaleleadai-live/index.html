Billing, refunds & cancellation

Subscriptions

ScaleLeadAI is sold as a monthly subscription. Your card is charged at the start of each billing period. You can cancel at any time from your account or by emailing us; cancellation stops future charges and your access continues until the end of the current paid period.

Free trial

New customers may start a 7-day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged.

If you're charged in error or are unhappy with your first payment, email scaleleadai.support@gmail.com within 14 days and we'll review your request for a refund of that charge.
